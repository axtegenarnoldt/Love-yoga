Welcome to yoga & mindfulness, a place for everyone, experienced and beginers



There are several different styles of yoga that range from gentle, meditative experiences to more physically intense formats. The mind-body exercise typically consists of doing sequences of physical poses combined with deep breathing techniques.

Improves strength.

Many yoga poses require you to support the weight of your own body in new ways. This will help you bulid strenght

Improves balance and flexibility.

Yoga can improve flexibility and mobility and increase range of motion. Over time, the ligaments, tendons and muscles lengthen, increasing elasticity.

Reduces anxiety.

improving posture and relaxing the muscles in a safe space can all help reduce anxiety.

Helps with stress relife.

Cortisol is often used to measure the stress response. Yoga practice has been demonstrated to reduce the levels of cortisol.

Improves heart health.

Yoga is a holistic practice that combines stress management and exercise. As a result, yoga is a unique two-in-one approach to improving heart health.
